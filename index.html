How Does It Work?

A step-by-step explanation is provided below to guide you through the process.

  1. Step 1

    Create a Profile

    Create your profile, and allow AbroadApp to guide you through the the process.

  2. Step 2

    Program and School Selection

    Choose your program with the assistance of our AI recommendation system.

  3. Step 3

    Submit Your Application

    Finalize your profile, upload documents, pay the application fee, and let the AbroadApp team handle your submission for the best shot at admission success.

  4. Step 4

    Receive Your Letter of Offer

    Upon review, if eligible and the program is open, you'll get a conditional offer letter.

  5. Step 5

    Pay Your Tuition Deposit.

    Finalize your enrollment by submitting the required tuition deposit payment as specified in the Letter of Offer.

  6. Step 6

    Receive your Letter of Acceptance.

    Upon receiving your tuition deposit and required documents as specified in the Letter of Offer, you'll swiftly receive your Letter of Acceptance.

  7. Step 7

    Start Your Visa Application

    Count on AbroadApp's certified immigration consultants to guide you through the study visa application process, ensuring the best chances of success.

  8. Step 8

    Register and Begin Your Program

    Confirm your study visa with AbroadApp, book your tickets, consult our experts for pre-departure and post-landing queries, enroll in classes, and embark on your academic journey abroad.

  9. Step 9

    Tap into AbroadApp's Career Coaching

    Upon arrival, connect with our career coach to receive guidance and support in navigating and thriving in your new international environment.
